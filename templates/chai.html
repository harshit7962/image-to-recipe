Requirements

  1. 1 Cup of Milk (250mL)
  2. 2 teaspoon Tea Powder
  3. 1/4 cup Water
  4. 3 teaspoons Sugar

Recipe

  1. Boil Water in a saucepan.
  2. Add sugar and tea powder in it and boil it for 3-4 minutes on medium flame.
  3. Add milk and boil it over medium flame for 6-7 minutes or until bubble starts to rise.
  4. Turn of the gas when you see a change in color of the tea from milky shade to brown shade.
  5. Strain tea in cups.
