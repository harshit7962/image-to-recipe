Image identified as Chapati!!!

Requirements

  1. 2 1/2 Cup whole wheat flour
  2. Water as required
  3. Salt as required
  4. 1 Tablespoon ghee

Recipe

  1. Put two cups of flour along with a cup of water, salt and ghee in it.
  2. Knead a smooth dough
  3. Flatten the dough balls
  4. Cook the Chapati
  5. Once heated enough, put the chapati on tha pan and cook from both sides.
